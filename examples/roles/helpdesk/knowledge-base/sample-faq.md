# Acme Corp Employee FAQ

## Time Off and Leave

### What is the PTO policy?

Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid time off (PTO) per calendar year.
PTO accrues at a rate of 1.67 days per month. Unused PTO carries over up to a
maximum of 5 days into the next calendar year. Employees in their first year
receive a prorated allocation based on their start date.

### How do I request time off?

Submit a time-off request through the HR portal at https://hr.example.com at
least 5 business days before the requested dates. Your manager will receive an
email notification and must approve the request. You will get a confirmation
email once approved. For urgent requests (e.g. family emergencies), contact your
manager directly and submit the portal request retroactively.

### What holidays does the company observe?

Acme Corp observes the following paid holidays:
- New Year's Day (January 1)
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day (third Monday in January)
- Presidents' Day (third Monday in February)
- Memorial Day (last Monday in May)
- Independence Day (July 4)
- Labor Day (first Monday in September)
- Thanksgiving Day (fourth Thursday in November)
- Day after Thanksgiving (fourth Friday in November)
- Christmas Eve (December 24)
- Christmas Day (December 25)

If a holiday falls on a weekend, it is observed on the nearest weekday.

## IT and Access

### How do I reset my password?

Visit https://sso.example.com/reset and enter your company email address. You
will receive a reset link valid for 30 minutes. If you are locked out of your
email, contact the IT Help Desk at it-support@example.com or call extension
4357 (HELP). Password requirements: minimum 12 characters, at least one
uppercase letter, one number, and one special character. Passwords expire every
90 days.

### How do I set up the VPN?

1. Download the Acme VPN client from https://vpn.example.com/download.
2. Install the client and launch it.
3. Enter the server address: `vpn.example.com`.
4. Sign in with your company SSO credentials.
5. Select "Acme-Corp-Full" from the connection profile dropdown.
6. Click Connect.

For troubleshooting, check that your firewall allows outbound connections on
ports 443 and 1194. If issues persist, contact it-support@example.com.

### How do I request new software?

Submit a software request through the IT Service Catalog at
https://it.example.com/catalog. Include the software name, business
justification, and the number of licenses needed. Requests are reviewed within
3 business days. Approved software is deployed via the company software center.
Unauthorized software installations are not permitted on company devices.

## Expenses and Reimbursement

### How do I submit an expense report?

1. Log in to the expense portal at https://expenses.example.com.
2. Click "New Report" and select the expense category.
3. Upload a photo or scan of each receipt.
4. Add a brief business justification for each line item.
5. Submit the report to your manager for approval.

Reports must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date. Approved
reimbursements are included in the next payroll cycle (typically within
2 pay periods).

### What are the travel expense limits?

- **Airfare**: Economy class for flights under 6 hours; premium economy for
  flights over 6 hours. Book at least 14 days in advance when possible.
- **Hotels**: Up to $200/night for domestic travel, $300/night for
  international travel. Use preferred hotels listed in the travel portal.
- **Meals**: Up to $75/day for domestic travel, $100/day for international
  travel. Alcohol is not reimbursable.
- **Ground transport**: Rideshare and taxis are reimbursable for business
  travel. Rental cars require pre-approval from your manager.

## Remote Work

### What is the remote work policy?

Acme Corp offers a hybrid work model. Employees may work remotely up to 3 days
per week, with a minimum of 2 days per week in the office. Remote work days
must be coordinated with your team to ensure adequate in-office coverage.
Fully remote arrangements require VP-level approval and are reviewed quarterly.

### What equipment is provided for remote work?

Remote employees receive a company laptop, external monitor, keyboard, and
mouse. A one-time $500 stipend is available for home office setup (desk, chair,
lighting). Submit the stipend request through the IT Service Catalog within your
first 90 days. Internet reimbursement of up to $75/month is available for
fully remote employees.

## Onboarding

### What should I expect on my first day?

On your first day, report to the front desk at 9:00 AM. You will receive your
badge, laptop, and a welcome packet. Your manager or onboarding buddy will give
you a tour of the office. The morning includes an HR orientation covering
benefits enrollment, company policies, and IT account setup. The afternoon is
reserved for team introductions and setting up your workstation.

### How long is the onboarding period?

The structured onboarding period is 90 days. During this time you will have
weekly check-ins with your manager and a 30/60/90-day review. Your onboarding
buddy is available throughout this period for questions. Benefits enrollment
must be completed within the first 30 days.
