---
name: summarizer
description: Summarize long text, articles, or documents into concise bullet points.
---

When summarizing content, follow these guidelines:

1. **Length**: Produce 3-7 bullet points for short content, up to 15 for long documents.
2. **Structure**: Lead each bullet with the key takeaway, then supporting detail.
3. **Tone**: Match the original content's tone (technical, casual, formal).
4. **Omissions**: Note if important context was left out due to length constraints.
