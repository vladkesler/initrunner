# Billing FAQ

## Billing Cycle

All paid plans are billed monthly on the anniversary of your upgrade date.
Invoices are emailed on the billing date and available under **Settings → Billing → Invoices**.

## Refund Policy

- Refund requests within **14 days** of a charge are eligible for a full refund.
- After 14 days, refunds are issued as account credit on a case-by-case basis.
- To request a refund, contact support with your invoice number.

## Accepted Payment Methods

- Visa, Mastercard, and American Express credit/debit cards.
- ACH bank transfer (US accounts, annual plans only).
- We do not accept PayPal, cryptocurrency, or wire transfers at this time.

## Upgrading or Downgrading

- Changes take effect immediately; you are billed a prorated amount for upgrades.
- Downgrades apply at the start of the next billing cycle.
- Navigate to **Settings → Billing → Change Plan** to modify your subscription.

## Failed Payments

If a payment fails, we retry automatically after 3, 5, and 7 days.
After three failed attempts, your account is downgraded to the Free tier.
Update your payment method under **Settings → Billing → Payment Method** to restore access.
