# Account Management

## Creating an Account

1. Visit the sign-up page at `/register`.
2. Enter your email address and choose a password (minimum 12 characters).
3. Verify your email by clicking the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
4. Complete your profile by adding your name and organization.

New accounts start on the **Free** tier with access to core features.

## Deleting an Account

1. Go to **Settings → Account → Delete Account**.
2. Enter your password to confirm.
3. All data is permanently removed within 30 days per our retention policy.

> **Note:** Deleting an account cannot be undone. Export your data first via **Settings → Export**.

## Resetting Your Password

1. Click **Forgot password?** on the login page.
2. Enter the email associated with your account.
3. Check your inbox for a reset link (valid for 1 hour).
4. Choose a new password and confirm.

If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

## Changing Your Email Address

1. Navigate to **Settings → Account → Email**.
2. Enter your new email address and current password.
3. A verification link is sent to the new address.
4. Click the link to confirm the change.

Your old email will no longer work for login after confirmation.
